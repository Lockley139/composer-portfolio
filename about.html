Bio

I'm a composer for video games and film, focused on narrative-driven music that supports story and emotion. My work ranges from full orchestral scores to intimate electronic palettes and interactive game audio.

Games & Film

I've composed for AAA and indie games, short films, documentaries, and trailers. I work closely with directors and creative teams to create music that feels integral to the experience—whether that's adaptive game music, cinematic themes, or sound design that blurs the line between score and soundscape.

Based between Los Angeles and London, I'm always open to new collaborations. When I'm not in the studio, I'm exploring modular synthesis, field recording, and live performance.
