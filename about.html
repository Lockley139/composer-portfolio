About

I write music for picture — film, television, and games. My work ranges from full orchestral scores to minimal electronic palettes, always in service of the story.

Based between Los Angeles and London, I collaborate with directors and producers worldwide. Recent credits include narrative features, documentary series, and AAA game trailers.

When I’m not in the studio, I’m usually exploring sound design, modular synthesis, or live performance. I believe the best scores are felt before they’re heard.
